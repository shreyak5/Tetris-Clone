<big>--- HOW TO PLAY ---</big><br>
The aim in Tetris is simple; <br>
You have to fill the lines at the bottom of the screen <br>
and stop the grid from filling up.<br>
The blocks fall from the top of the screen.<br>
You can move them around and rotate them.<br><br>

<big>--- CONTROLS ---</big><br>
Move Right - Right Arrow <br>
Move Left - Left Arrow <br>
Rotate Right - D or Up Arrow <br>
Rotate Left - A <br>
Hard Drop - Spacebar <br>
Soft Drop - Down Arrow <br><br>

<big>--- POINTS ---</big> <br>
Clear one line : +100 <br>
Clear two lines: +200 <br>
Clear three lines: +300 <br>
Clear four lines: +400 <br>
